History

Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology (MBCET) was established in July 2002 by the Malankara Catholic Church. Affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE, the college has grown into one of the most respected institutions in Kerala. MBCET is known for academic excellence, ethical values, and contributions to technological advancement.
